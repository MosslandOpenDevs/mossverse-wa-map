## village.tmj

- Base / starter map for this repository

- Preserve the upstream `mapCopyright` metadata embedded in `village.tmj`
- Do not treat the `village.tmj` attribution notice as interchangeable with the starter-kit attribution notice
- Review upstream provenance and asset rights before redistributing modified versions

## Editing Guidance

- When editing maps in Tiled, keep existing map properties such as `mapCopyright`, `mapName`, `mapImage`, and `script`
- When adding new third-party maps or assets, document them here and keep their notices in the corresponding `.tmj` files
